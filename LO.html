Learning Objective:

  1. To measure voltage and current in a DC circuit for each element.
  2. To calculate analytically V and I.
  3. Compare analytical and practical values.
  4. Verify KCL and KVL.
  5. To apply concepts of KCL and KVL in network theorems.
